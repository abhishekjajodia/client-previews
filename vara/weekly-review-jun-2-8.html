05What we learned

Takeaways from this week's numbers that shape what we do next.

BudgetSpend is concentrated on a high-volume / low-efficiency ad. Shifting budget to the ₹2.3–2.8 winners lifts efficiency without spending more.
CreativeSome ads get lots of clicks but few sales (3.45% click rate, ₹1.12 return). Hook works, close doesn't — keep the hooks, strengthen offer/proof.
StructureCold prospecting was the leak (₹1.21). Retiring it + the warm-audience packs should lift Meta efficiency next week — the fix for the dip Prithvi flagged.
GooglePMax is efficient (₹3.29) but under-fed — only ₹30K/wk and one active asset set. Refresh images + lift the cap.
RetentionThe cross-sell base is the biggest lifetime-value lever: moving a buyer to both products nearly triples their value (₹7.8K → ₹20.9K). 5,189 customers are in that pipeline — the subscription + cross-sell broadcast are how we convert them.

08New Meta features to test

The new options Prithvi flagged, plus a wider audit — in priority order.

Customer list — YES, but how mattersFeed the full list in three roles: a signal to find similar buyers, an exclusion so we don't re-sell existing customers, and a target for cross-sell. Honest note: Klaviyo likely already syncs most of this — the gain is control + freshness, not a brand-new capability. We'll cap it so Meta doesn't over-spend on warm buyers.
